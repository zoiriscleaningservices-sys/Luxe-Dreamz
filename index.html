Our locations

Entertainment Center: Surge Entertainment delivers an unmatched experience with a variety of cutting-edge attractions, including an arcade, bowling, laser tag, karting, a full bar, restaurant and more! Whether you’re seeking thrilling activities, immersive entertainment, or a memorable event, we provide a high-energy environment thats perfect for friends, families, and fun for all ages.

Adventure Park: Surge Adventure Park is an action-packed indoor trampoline park featuring a variety of exciting attractions, including dodgeball, a zip line, climbing walls, a challenging ninja course and more. With something for everyone, it’s the ultimate destination for fun, fitness, parties, & events.
